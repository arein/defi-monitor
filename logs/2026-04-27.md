### Headline
SingularityFinance.ai loses ~$413K from its dynBaseUSDCv3 vault on Base after a months-old admin misconfiguration registered Uniswap V3 oracle routes against fee tier 42, a tier the V3 factory does not allow.

### Incidents
**SingularityFinance.ai dynBaseUSDCv3 vault drain (~$413K).** On 2026-01-19 the protocol admin registered six yield-token oracle routes with a Uniswap V3 fee tier of 42. Uniswap V3 only enables fee tiers 100, 500, 3000, and 10000, so `factory.getPool(USDC, X, 42)` returned `address(0)` for every token, silently killing the direct price path. The WETH-fallback pools that did exist had zero liquidity, so `VaultTokensLib.totalAssets()` only counted the ~$100 idle USDC. The attacker flash-loaned 100k USDC from Morpho, deposited into the vault to mint ~99.99% of supply at the broken ratio, then redeemed for a proportional cut of every actual token balance, draining the underlying yield tokens. Reported by Icalurus$ from preliminary research, with the official post-mortem to follow ([announcement](https://t.me/Singularity_Fi/262366), [exploit tx](https://basescan.org/tx/0x00b949bc3ed3edb58b04faedfbd8eb1db2edceae761382e80fe012919f8d3732), [setUniV3fee=42 tx](https://basescan.org/tx/0x2df0be7a17bd69a2f732c1396796690240aecdfaf13b0a8f60f49f95a8dbe150), [DefimonAlerts](https://x.com/DefimonAlerts/status/2048698708309705069)).
