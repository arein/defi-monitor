## Digest

_regenerated 2026-04-20T19:55:00Z from 444 messages (replay)_

Kelp DAO rsETH was exploited for roughly $290M via a forged LayerZero message from the Unichain peer; the 1/1 DVN config was the immediate cause but the day's deeper revelation was that 24 of 26 rsETH OFT pathways used the same LayerZero Labs default, meaning this was a latent systemic vulnerability across most of LZ-deployed DeFi.

### Incidents

- **Kelp DAO rsETH (exploit, critical)**: forged cross-chain message released ~115k rsETH on Ethereum mainnet without a corresponding source-side burn; attacker used it as Aave collateral to max-borrow WETH until the pool hit full utilization. Kelp had paused rsETH contracts and zeroed LTV before the thread even coalesced (noted by Tom, Icalurus). banteg's forensic [gist](https://gist.github.com/banteg/705d0284513b74ad20f61d90f5b5de62) became the reference write-up; m4rio, geo, and Syko pinned the cause to a compromised LayerZero Labs DVN (EOA `0x589d...236b`) that signed a spoofed quorum message. Hawre drew the comparison to Hyperbridge's same-playbook exploit. Fund flow: Tornado → FixedFloat (Icalurus traced; heraldur flagged the timing makes the funder-via-tornado theory inverted). Andrey estimated ~$75.9M in probable Aave bad debt ([thread](https://x.com/andrENsobol/status/2045737556344545578)); Tim Daubenschütz's earlier napkin math put socialized haircut at 5-9% if umbrella absorbs the loss.
- **LayerZero infra (systemic, high)**: Tay enumerated the 2/2 DVN-configured OFTs downstream of the same architecture (USDT0, Ethena USDe, Sky, etherfi weETH, cmETH, pufETH, cUSD, rUSD, iUSD, AUSD, USDai); Primo from LayerZero confirmed USDT0, Ethena, Sky, and etherfi proactively paused. James Prestwich published [Zero Validation](https://prestwich.substack.com/p/zero-validation) asserting a 2/3 LayerZero admin multisig can overwrite DVN config on apps that didn't explicitly opt out, which Primo disputed in-channel. Anatol found the [LayerZero Labs gasolina-aws CDK](https://github.com/LayerZero-Labs/gasolina-aws/blob/main/cdk/gasolina/lib/gasolinaApi.ts#L91) creates all three DVN KMS keys in a single AWS account and IAM role by default, collapsing nominally 3-of-N setups into effectively 1-of-N.

### Discussion

Aave exposure debate: Anatol argued that because the rsETH on Aave is still backed by real ETH on mainnet, Aave shouldn't be liable for umbrella socialization; Ape countered that the real-ETH backing is fungible against the unbacked L2 supply, so Aave's users are underwater de facto even if Aave did nothing wrong. Architectural thread ran on light-client validation (Jag advocating MMR-root verification), MPC attestation (Rob proposing ZK endpoints for "is this key an MPC"; James noting no ZKP can prove non-existence of a backdoor key), and fail-closed upgrade models (blocked by LZ's current upgradeability). Coinspect dropped [artifacts from the eth.limo DNSSEC hijack](https://github.com/coinspect/snippets) as a side-note.

Also mentioned: Hudson's moderator warning about a Molly impersonator, griff.eth's emoji poll on haircut distribution (all rsETH holders vs L2-only), and an extended flame between Tay, James, Jayen and Yannick about learning-vs-blame culture that absorbed maybe 30 messages.
